Ports

As soon as the gang-plank touches the quay-side, the terrors of the deep zee lighten. Walk taller: smile more readily

Ports are the oh so rare safe harbours on a largely hostile and treacherous sea. They allow you to stock up on supplies and fuel, and lower your terror, and they’re also where most of the stories play out.

Fuel and supplies

The success of a journey lies in planning your route well – to help with that, I’ve compiled a table that compares the price of fuel and supplies at each port, so you don’t get any unpleasant surprises - such as finding that the port where you planned to stock up on fuel sells it at three times the average price, or that the place you barely managed to get to on the edge of starvation doesn’t sell supplies at all.
The table doesn’t contain ports without shops. The prices are all in echoes, unless otherwise specified.

Regions

Ports don’t always stay in place however, and you might be surprised to find a port you’ve discovered with one captain is somewhere entirely different the next time around. The map is generated differently each time you start anew, but named locations stay in certain regions of the map, so you may still have a rough sense of where to find each port.
